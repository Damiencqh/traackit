Traackit.

Quiet daily photos that become a story. One tap a day; a timelapse at the end. No streaks, no social, no clutter — just the slow proof that something is changing.

One screen, one job

The home is a list — your traacks, the days behind them, and whether today is done. Everything else hides until needed.

Align, don't aim

A faint silhouette over the camera holds your framing steady across weeks. Face, torso, plant, or a shape you draw yourself.

Soft reminders

One push at the time you choose. Skip a day without guilt — there are no streaks to break, just a record that keeps building.
